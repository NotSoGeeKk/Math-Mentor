## Sequences & Series

### Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- nth term: `a_n = a + (n-1)d`
- Sum of n terms: `S_n = n/2 * [2a + (n-1)d]` or `S_n = n/2 * (a + l)`
- Common difference: `d = a_2 - a_1`

### Geometric Progression (GP)
- nth term: `a_n = a * r^(n-1)`
- Sum of n terms: `S_n = a(r^n - 1)/(r - 1)` for `r ≠ 1`
- Sum to infinity (|r| < 1): `S_∞ = a / (1 - r)`

### Harmonic Progression (HP)
A sequence is HP if reciprocals form an AP.
There is no direct sum formula — convert to AP of reciprocals.

### AM-GM-HM Inequality
For positive reals a, b:
- AM = `(a + b) / 2`
- GM = `√(ab)`
- HM = `2ab / (a + b)`
- `AM ≥ GM ≥ HM`, equality iff `a = b`

### Sum of Powers
- `Σ k (k=1 to n) = n(n+1)/2`
- `Σ k² (k=1 to n) = n(n+1)(2n+1)/6`
- `Σ k³ (k=1 to n) = [n(n+1)/2]²`
